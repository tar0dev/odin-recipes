Delicious Chicken and Rice

Link

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Brocolli
  • Rice (White or Brown)

Seasoning

  • 1/2 tsp Parika
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp brown or granulated sugar

Steps for cooking this dish

  1. First cook rice because that takes the longest
  2. Season the chicken and cook it in a hot skillet or heavy duty pan
  3. Steam the broccoli in a stove top pot in boiling water.
